# Chapter 2 Image Generation Prompts (Final Used)

This document contains the actual prompts used to generate the educational diagrams for Chapter 2.
**Style:** Stick figure / XKCD-style (Simple black line drawings on white background).

---

## 01. Listening & Give/Take Focus
**Filename:** `01_listening_give_take.png`

**Prompt:**
```
Create a simple educational diagram using stick figure style (xkcd-like). Two stick figures on a stage. Left figure: speaking with open mouth, animated gesture, labeled "ГОВОРИТ" (Speaking) above. Right figure: listening attentively with hand cupped to ear, labeled "СЛУШАЕТ" (Listening) above. A curved arrow from the listening figure to the speaking figure labeled "ДАВАТЬ ФОКУС" (Give Focus) - showing that the listener gives focus to the speaker. A curved arrow from the speaking figure to the listening figure labeled "БРАТЬ ФОКУС" (Take Focus) - showing that the speaker takes focus. Use simple black line drawings on white background. Clean, minimal style. Clear sans-serif Cyrillic typography for labels. Simple stage floor line. Educational and clear. 4:3 aspect ratio.
```
